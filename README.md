# Downsell - Bank Statement Analyzer

Downsell is a free, client-side financial analysis tool that helps you understand your spending patterns and identify recurring subscriptions from your bank statements.

## What It Does

Upload your bank statement CSV files and Downsell will:

- **Detect Recurring Subscriptions:** Automatically identifies recurring payments and subscriptions from your transaction history
- **Visualize Spending Patterns:** See your spending broken down by subscriptions, with charts showing trends over time
- **Track Multiple Accounts:** Upload CSVs from multiple bank accounts (Revolut, AIB, Bank of Ireland) and view them together
- **Identify Savings Opportunities:** Find high-confidence subscriptions that may be candidates for cancellation or switching
- **Generate Reports:** Download detailed PDF reports of your financial analysis
- **Privacy-First:** All processing happens entirely in your browser. Your financial data never leaves your device.

## Supported Banks

Downsell currently supports CSV exports from:
- **Revolut**
- **AIB** (Allied Irish Banks)
- **Bank of Ireland**

We're expanding to support more banks in the coming weeks.

## Key Features

- **Automatic Subscription Detection:** Identifies recurring outgoing payments, ignoring transfers and exchanges
- **Multi-Bank Support:** Upload and merge CSVs from different banks for a unified view
- **Spending Visualizations:** Charts showing subscription spending over time, top subscriptions by spend, and payment frequency patterns
- **Transaction History:** View all transactions in a searchable, filterable table with color-coded transaction types
- **Account Tracking:** See which accounts (AIB-1, REV-1, BOI-1, etc.) each subscription appears in
- **Actionable Insights:** Identify subscriptions with consistent pricing patterns that may be good candidates for optimization
- **PDF Reports:** Download comprehensive reports with charts and analysis

## Privacy & Security

All analysis happens entirely on your device. Nothing is stored on our servers or sent anywhere. Your financial data never leaves your browser. This is a free public tool designed to help everyone understand their finances better.
